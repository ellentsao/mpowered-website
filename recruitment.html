How to Become Part of MPowered!

  • Mass Meetings

    Attend one of our mass meetings for more information on our recruitment process, to meet our members, and see what MPowered is all about.

    Friday, 9/3 @ 7pm

    Tuesday, 9/7 @ 7pm

    Wednesday, 9/8 @ 7pm

  • Submit an Application

    We want to get to know you and why you’re interested in MPowered! This application is one of the most important ways we get to do that.

    Applications are due Saturday, 9/11, at 11:59 PM.

  • Interviews

    After the last MPowered Mass Meeting, you’ll receive an email asking you to sign up to interview with up to three of our six internal teams. More detailed instructions will be sent in that email.

  • Rankings

    After all interviews are complete, you’ll rank the teams you interviewed with in order of preference, and they will do the same with all of their potential candidates. We’ll place you on the best possible team for everyone involved.
