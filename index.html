Who We Are

We are the University of Michigan's oldest and largest entrepreneurship organization. We bring together distinguished companies and outstanding students to lead groundbreaking initiatives across campus. We’re currently building a diverse team of self-starters, big thinkers, and innovators, and we want you to join us.

MPowered is broken up into six smaller teams. When you join our organization, you’re not only joining the entire MPowered community, but you’re placed on one of these teams based on your application, interviews, individual skills, and, most importantly, your personal preferences. These teams launch initiatives and events that engage the U-M and Ann Arbor startup ecosystem in unique ways.

Innovation Challenge is a yearly entrepreneurial game that challenges participants from all schools and colleges to design, build, plan and execute, utilizing teamwork and networking. Teams display their entrepreneurial knowledge to put themselves at an advantage in order to complete challenges set to them. The students race against time to with their sights on the prize!

MTank is a project similar to the reality TV show Shark Tank, where startups come to pitch their ideas, get live feedback, and guidance from judges, and compete for capital from Venture Capitalists. This event is presented by MPowered and changes the game of pitch competition. MTank adds entertainment to the playing field, drawing in a crowd and resulting in huge exposure for the startups.

TrailBlaze brings U-M’s diverse skills and resources to low-income, first-generation middle school students to unite the gaps between school curriculum and other vast opportunities to learn. Our program emphasizes that innovation is not limited to STEM, but can be found and created in unique forms through a series of workshops and activities. Talent members will add on to their experiences in logistics, marketing, and recruiting.

For the 2022 event, our theme is "Spark Your Future!" We will facilitate educational and creative activities to provide exposure to a wide variety of fields. MPowered Consulting is a pro bono venture support organization focused on solving real business challenges for startups. We complete short term projects for entrepreneurs to support the entrepreneurial community. We are united by our desire to solve complex business problems and make a positive impact on our community. We pride ourselves in our ability to integrate into startups’ existing teams and how rapidly we can complete projects for our partners.

The Marketing team works to ensure a unified MPowered brand.

Our team is split into two main sub-teams: Marketing and Creative. Marketing is tasked with providing the project teams with a detailed marketing strategy, developing resources to achieve our marketing goals as an org, and providing a structure to empower our continuously expanding MPowered brand. Creative is tasked with providing the necessary graphics and materials to supplement the events and many projects we have throughout the year.

Join us to gain valuable firsthand marketing and/or creative experience by building a presence across campus, online, at all of our events, and beyond.
